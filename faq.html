Faq

Why should I get involved?

Our event organizers increase the visibility of their programs and partners, see new attendees, and help show support for a growing community. Oh, and because you love the Philly tech community!

At what time should my event take place?

Events will take place throughout each day. We promise to curate the calendar as best as possible to make sure that your event gets noticed. When submitting your event idea, consider time slots and the convenience of the audience you’re hoping to reach.

Where should my event take place?

Virtually! Online. Though if you have a unique and safe idea for an outdoor, small-group activation that follows local, state, and federal guidelines, you’re welcome to submit and we will assess.

What are Recommended time slots?

  • Morning: 8:00 a.m. - 10:00 a.m.
  • Late Morning: 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.
  • Lunch: 12:00 p.m. - 2:00 p.m.
  • Afternoon: 2:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
  • Happy Hour: 5:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m.
  • Evening: 7:00 p.m. - 12:00 a.m
